## Problem Statement
Traditional inventory systems lack real-time updates, predictive insights, and efficient management of stock, expiry, and sales. This leads to stockouts, overstocking, and manual effort in tracking inventory.

## Data Structures Used
- HashMap: Stores products using Product ID for constant-time access and updates.
- MinHeap (Stock): Maintains products by quantity to identify low stock efficiently.
- MinHeap (Expiry): Tracks products by expiry date and supports automatic removal.
- Stack: Enables undo of the most recent billing transactions.
- AVL Tree: Supports efficient range queries on product attributes.
- TreeMap: Stores date-wise sales data for ordered retrieval and analytics.
- ArrayList with Sliding Window: Computes sales velocity and stockout prediction.
- Moving Average: Used for demand forecasting in analytics.
